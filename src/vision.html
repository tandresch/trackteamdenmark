OUR VISION

Our mission is to strengthen Danish track cycling by creating opportunities for riders, partners and future talent.

By establishing the first-ever Danish commercial track cycling team, we aim to continue developing track cycling in Denmark and on the international stage. With the World Championships on home soil in 2024 and the strong success of the Danish national track team, it was the right time to launch this project in 2025 and build further on the momentum within the Danish track cycling scene.

The team partly serves as an extension of the national track team, working closely together to strengthen depth and performance at the highest level of Danish track cycling. It also functions as an independent platform, providing opportunities for riders outside the national team to pursue a career in track cycling.

We bridge business and elite cycling - making it easy for companies to plug into the sport and turn partnerships into real visibility, engagement, and value.

Our goal for the second season is to continue growing and build on the strong foundation we established in 2025. Both collective and individual development remain at the heart of our approach, which is why each rider will continue to receive a personalized program tailored to their goals.
