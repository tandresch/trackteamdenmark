THE ELITE

Track Team Denmark brings together some of the world's most decorated track cyclists with Olympic, World, and European medals. Our mission is to build on these successes and push each rider even further, reaching new heights on the international stage.

Our vision

OUR VISION

By establishing the first ever Danish track cycling team we want to develop track cycling in Denmark but also on the international stage. With the World Championships on home soil in 2024 and the very successful danish national track team it was the right time to start this project in 2025 and support the development of the Danish track scene.

The team will act as an extension of the national track team, working closely together to strengthen the performance width at the top of Danish track cycling. At the same time it offers the opportunity to riders who are not part of the national team, to pursue a career in track cycling.

The team makes it easy for companies to enter the sport. Due to the team’s media presence, both sides benefit from investments which makes partnerships interesting.

Our goal for the second season is to continue growing and build on the strong foundation we established in 2025. Both collective and individual development remain at the heart of our approach, which is why each rider will continue to receive a personalized program tailored to their goals.

EUROPEAN CHAMPIONSHIPS

Gold, silver and a world record for our TTD riders

March 5, 2026

The European Championships were a great success for the Danish team and our Track Team Denmark riders. In total, five riders from our team represented their countries in Konya, Turkey.

Lasse Norman Leth, Robin Skivild, and Rasmus Lund claimed the European title in the team pursuit with an outstanding performance, even going under the world record.
